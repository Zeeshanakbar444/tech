Quotes have been utilized for centuries as a way to remember what would likely be otherwise fleeting words in the wind.

The act of codifying the spoken word into tangible written language allows us as the human race to create and remember the sentiment surrounding statements. It is not far-fetched to assume that nearly every person across the globe has an influential quote that they encounter each day. Whether it be in their bedroom, kitchen, or even a phone background, humans and quotes go hand-in-hand.

Though the majority of people associate quotations with matters of personal significance, quotes can be applied to every situation. Digital marketing is no exception to the influence of quotations, and we here at e9digital have compiled a list of the 20 best quotes about digital marketing. We hope our list of the best digital marketing quotes can help inspire others in the digital marketing industry in the same way that they have inspired us here at e9digital.

20 Best Digital Marketing Quotes

1

“It’s not what you sell that matters as much as how you sell it!”

- Brian Halligan

We would have been remiss to leave this off the list of best digital marketing quotes. Though this quote extends beyond the confines of digital marketing, the concept rings true for the industry. There are countless companies that provide similar digital marketing services, but the selling of the service is how you can strive as a business.

2

“The customer is not a moron; she is your wife.”

- David Ogilvy

In today’s age, customers are more informed than ever before. Companies must acknowledge this increased level of knowledge to remain afloat.

3

“Ignoring online marketing is like opening a business but not telling anyone.”

- KB Marketing Agency

One of our favorite digital marketing quotes for business, this quote cuts straight to the point that online marketing is a quintessential part of the business world today.

4

“Content is not king, but a president elected by the votes of those whom it aims to rule.”

- Raheel Farooq

Everyone has heard that “content is king”. However, we prefer this more nuanced definition in which only content chosen by the consumer rules the marketplace.

5

“The best marketing doesn’t feel like marketing.”

- Tom Fishburne

Fishburne provides one of the best quotes about digital marketing that you’ll ever read.

6

“90% trust peers on social networks; only 15-18% trust brands.”

- Danny Brown

Think about personal experiences. How often have you been hesitant to purchase a new product purely based on an advertisement? In today’s digital arena, the power of public opinion remains unmatched.

7

“Business has only two functions- marketing and innovation.”

- Milan Kundera

Another straight to the point digital marketing quote for business that resonates heavily today and demonstrates just how important marketing is in the world.

8

“What separates good content from great content is a willingness to take risks and push the envelope.”

- Brian Halligan

Prevalent across nearly every aspect of life, this quote emphasizes how essential taking risks is in content creation.

9

“Stop interrupting what people are interested in and be what people are interested in.”

- Craig Davis

The above quote gives digital marketers something to strive for in their innovations and content creation.

10

“Either write something worth reading or do something worth writing about.”

- Benjamin Franklin

Though certainly not intended to be one of the best quotes about digital marketing, Benjamin Franklin sentiment on written word is still applicable hundreds of years later.

11

“It’s important to remember your competitor is only one mouse click away.”

- Douglas Warner III

Never before has competition been so fierce in the business world. With the evolution of technology, consumers can switch to a competitor in seconds.

12

“Your brand is what people say about you when you’re not in the room.”

- Jeff Bezos

Bezos emphasizes the active nature of consumers in his above quote. Companies must make lasting positive impressions that last beyond a consumers initial interaction.

13

“How to write a good email: 1. Write your email 2. Delete most of it 3. Send.”

- Dan Munz

Another one of the best digital marketing quotes due to its brevity and accuracy. Munz makes a statement on the necessity for concise communication and short consumer attention span.

14

“Good marketing makes the company look smart. Great marketing makes the customer feel smart.”

- Joe Chernov

Here at e9, we strive to not only market but to inform our customers.

15

“If you take a risk and it doesn’t go as planned, welcome to the club.”

- Fran Hauser

A universal quote that also applies well to the digital marketing industry. Failure is a part of taking risks and is necessary to succeed in the long run.

16

“A brand is no longer what we tell the consumer it is- it is what consumers tell each other it is.”

- Scott Cook

Similar to Jeff Bezos’s quote above, the active nature of the consumer is reiterated by Cook.

17

“When creating content, be the best answer on the internet.”

- Andy Crestodina

Crestodina gives digital marketers everywhere something to strive for in their content creation.

18

“Advertising brings in the customers, but it is your job to keep them buying from you.”

- Chet Holmes

Advertising is only the first step of a successful customer interaction. Even after a company has completed a sale, the work is still ongoing to create return customers.

19

“In the time it took you to read this sentence 20 million emails have been sent.”

- John Watton

A statement on the fast paced environment that we live in today, Watton conveys that millions of business interactions occur in a matter of seconds which makes it crucial to create quality interactions with customers.

20

“Content is fire; social media is gasoline.”

- Jay Baer

Baer provides another of the best digital marketing quotes with this analogy. Baer states that creating content is only the spark that is then accelerated by consumers in the social media landscape.

We here at e9digital find inspiration from these quotes on almost a daily basis.
