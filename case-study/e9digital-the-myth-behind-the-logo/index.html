e9digital

The myth behind the logo

In 2008, founder/CEO Conrad Strabone had secured business and was set to launch his digital agency. Just one problem. No name, until…inspiration struck. After a few celebratory glasses of wine, e9 came to him in a dream. Not just the name, but a logo design that embodied balance through the repetition of shapes.
